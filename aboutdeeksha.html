VISION & MISSION


      Deeksha creation Society was founded in year 2002. It is an altruistic organization working for the social
   upliftment through high quality education which may produce not only professionals, but really good
   human being cherishing practicing and propagating moral, ethical and spiritual values in their thought,
   speech and action. The guiding principle of the society is to serve the needy and the surfing because as
   Tagore said:
       �Here is thy footsore and there rest thy feet where live the poorest and lowliest and lost.�

Our Vision:

  • To nurture thought leaders and practitioners through inventive education.

Our Mission:

  • Focus on inventive education by offering practical, innovative and technology driven programs.
  • Develop passion for learning and creative thinking and values in rapidly evolving economic and
    social environment.
  • Contribute significantly to growth by preparing graduates with high mindset.
  • Build intellectual capital through faculty development, research, consultancy and publication.
  • Develop alumni network of mutual benefit and keep alumni update through continuous learning
    and meeting.

Our Values:

  • Breakthrough Thinking � We foster academic rigor in an environment conductive to innovation.
  • Result Oriented, Process Driven work Ethic � We adopt dynamic quality processes to ensure accountability and exceptional performances.
  • Passion � The soul of RNT blossoms in our heart, mind and body.
